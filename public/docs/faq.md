# FriendRank FAQ

Concise answers to common questions about FriendRank. Website: https://friendrank.app

## What is FriendRank?

FriendRank is a free multiplayer social voting game. A host creates a game with friend names, shares a link, and friends vote on humorous personality roles. When enough votes are in, the group unlocks ranked results and a shareable group story.

## Is voting anonymous?

Yes, in practice. FriendRank does not require accounts, and individual ballots are not shown to the group. Votes are aggregated into category winners and narrative results. Each device uses a browser-local voter token so the same person cannot vote twice in one game.

## Do I need an account?

No. You can create a game, vote, and view results without signing up, email, or password.

## Can I create multiple games?

Yes. Each visit to the homepage can start a new game with its own share link and results. Games are independent.

## Can I add my own categories?

Yes. When creating a game, you can enter up to three custom category prompts (for example “Most likely to ghost the group chat”). Each game uses five categories total; custom entries fill slots and the rest come from FriendRank’s built-in library.

## How many people can vote?

Each game supports two to eight friend names. Results unlock after enough distinct votes are collected—typically at least as many votes as there are friends in the game, with a minimum of two votes.

## How many friends can I add to a game?

You can list up to eight names when creating a game. At least two names are required.

## Can I play on mobile?

Yes. FriendRank runs in the mobile browser. The voting flow, progress indicators, and results are designed for phone screens. No app install is required.

## How long does it take?

Creating a game takes under a minute. Voting is five quick taps—one per category. Total group time depends on how fast friends open the link; many groups finish in a few minutes once everyone votes.

## Can I share through WhatsApp?

Yes. Copy the game link from the homepage after creation and paste it into WhatsApp, iMessage, Discord, or any chat app. The link format is `https://friendrank.app/game/{share_code}`.

## When do results unlock?

Results unlock when the vote threshold is met. For new games, that is at least two votes and scales with group size (matching the number of friends listed, minimum two). A progress indicator shows votes collected versus required.

## Can I see results before everyone votes?

No. The full results page stays locked until the vote threshold is reached. Partial progress is visible on the game page.

## Can I replay the same game?

The same game link remains available. After results unlock, anyone with the link can view them. To run a fresh round with new questions and votes, create a new game on the homepage.

## What tones can I choose?

At game creation you pick one tone: Funny, Savage but friendly, Wholesome, or Chaotic. Tone shapes how questions and results are worded.

## What are vibe tags?

Optional tags (up to three) such as Chaotic, College, Discord, or Party. They help personalize question flavor and results narrative for your group context.

## What happens after I vote?

After submitting all five votes, you see confirmation that your votes are recorded. You can share the link with friends who have not voted yet. When results unlock, reopen the same link to view them.

## Is FriendRank free?

Yes. FriendRank is free to use in the browser at https://friendrank.app.

## Do voters need the friend list in advance?

The host enters names when creating the game. Voters see those names as options on each question. Everyone in the list should be someone the group recognizes.

## Can one person vote for themselves?

The voting UI lists the friends from the game setup. Who each voter picks is not revealed individually; only aggregated outcomes appear in results.

## Is my game link private?

Game links are unlisted—they work for anyone who has the URL. Treat the share link like a private invite. Do not post it publicly if you want a closed group.

## What appears in the results?

Unlocked results include category rankings, group verdict narrative, vibe summary, dangerous combo pairings, group reputation text, and a highlight card you can share or download.

## Where can I learn about categories?

See the full built-in category reference at https://friendrank.app/docs/categories.md and the product overview at https://friendrank.app/about-friendrank.md.
